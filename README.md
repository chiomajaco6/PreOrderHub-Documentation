# 🚀 PreOrderHub

> **A Campaign-Based Preorder Management Platform for WhatsApp Businesses**

PreOrderHub is a web-based platform designed to simplify the management of preorder businesses that operate primarily through WhatsApp. It automates product management, customer orders, installment payments, shipping fee management, and shipment tracking while providing an intuitive dashboard for both customers and business owners.

Unlike conventional e-commerce platforms, PreOrderHub is specifically designed for businesses that run **time-bound preorder campaigns**, accept **installment payments**, and calculate **shipping fees after campaign closure**.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📅 Campaign-based preorder management
- 🛍️ Product catalogue with categories
- 🛒 Shopping cart and checkout
- 💰 Installment payment management
- 📊 Automatic outstanding balance calculation
- 🚚 Shipping fee management
- 📦 Shipment tracking
- 👤 Customer dashboard
- ⚙️ Administrator dashboard
- 📈 Sales reports and analytics
- 🔍 Product sourcing request with image upload
- ✅ Digital acceptance of preorder terms and conditions

---

## 🏗️ Technology Stack

The implementation is planned using:

- **Backend:** Django (Python)
- **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5, JavaScript
- **Database:** PostgreSQL
- **Charts:** Chart.js
- **Cloud Hosting:** Render / DigitalOcean / AWS
- **Media Storage:** Cloudinary
